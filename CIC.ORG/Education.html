Education

At CIC-UG, we are dedicated to empowering refugee youths by providing them with practical skills in domestic electrical installation.
Our comprehensive training program is designed to equip these young individuals with the knowledge and expertise needed to excel in the electrical trade.
Through hands-on learning experiences and industry-relevant curriculum, we aim to enhance their employability and open doors to sustainable career opportunities in the electrical sector.

Practical Training

Our program emphasizes hands-on training, allowing students to gain real-world experience in domestic electrical installation.
Students work on actual projects, learning to install wiring, outlets, switches, and lighting fixtures under the guidance of experienced instructors.

Industry-Relevant Curriculum

We offer a curriculum that is aligned with industry standards, ensuring that our students are well-prepared for the job market.
Our courses cover essential topics such as wiring, safety protocols, and electrical systems installation.

Career Support

We provide career guidance and support to help our graduates find employment opportunities in the electrical trade.
Our dedicated team works closely with local employers to identify job openings and connect graduates with potential employers.
