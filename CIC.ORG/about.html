About Us

Our History

Creative Innovation Centre (CIC) is a youth-led organization based in Ofua III within the Rhino Camp Refugee Settlement in Uganda. Established in 2020 by a collective of creative electronics enthusiasts passionate about digital literacy and technology, CICâ€™s main aim is to provide a collaborative platform for generating and sharing innovative ideas by engaging youths in peer-to-peer learning, and mentorship programs centered on education and practical vocational skills, helping them navigate their career paths more effectively in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) related fields. Furthermore, CIC advocates for holistic and sustainable youth development by utilizing electronic to enhance technological understanding and promote environmental conservation through e waste management.

Mission & Vision

Mission:CIC's mission is to design and implement integrated solutions that uplift communities by: Cultivating Future Leaders: Through targeted youth empowerment and accessible education programs that build essential skills and foster civic engagement. Driving Sustainable Innovation: By applying engineering and agri-tech principles to develop effective, localized systems for food security, clean water, and sanitation. Advancing Community Well-being: Through holistic health initiatives and advocating for environmental justice to ensure equitable access to safe and thriving living spaces.

Vision: To be the leading catalyst for sustainable community transformation, empowering youth and harnessing the synergy of education, technology, and environmental stewardship to cultivate resilient, equitable, and thriving futures globally.
