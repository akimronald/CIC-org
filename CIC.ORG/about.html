About Davizo

Our History

Founded in 2005, Davizo started as a single station in Juba with a commitment to quality service. Over the years, we've grown into a leading regional provider of fuel and energy solutions, always staying true to our core values.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide reliable, high-quality energy solutions with exceptional customer service, powering communities and businesses across South Sudan.

Vision: To be the most trusted and innovative energy partner, leading the way in sustainability and customer satisfaction.

Certifications

We are ISO 9001:2015 certified, ensuring the highest standards of quality management in all our operations.

Safety & Environment

Committed to the highest safety protocols and environmentally responsible practices to protect our people and our planet.
