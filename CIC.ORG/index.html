Education

At CIC-UG, we are dedicated to empowering refugee youths by providing them with practical skills in domestic electrical installation.
Our comprehensive training program is designed to equip these young individuals with the knowledge and expertise needed to excel in the electrical trade.
Through hands-on learning experiences and industry-relevant curriculum, we aim to enhance their employability and open doors to sustainable career opportunities in the electrical sector.

Agri-tech

At CIC-UG, we are committed to advancing agricultural practices through innovative technology solutions.
Our Agri-tech program focuses on equipping individuals with the skills needed to thrive in the evolving agricultural landscape.
By integrating technology into farming practices, we aim to enhance productivity, sustainability, and food security.

Environmental justice

At CIC-UG, we are dedicated to promoting environmental justice through education and community engagement.
Our Environmental Justice program aims to raise awareness about environmental issues affecting marginalized communities and empower individuals to advocate for equitable solutions.
By fostering a deeper understanding of the intersection between environmental sustainability and social justice, we strive to create a more inclusive and resilient future for all.
